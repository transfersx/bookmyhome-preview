Book My Home should launch a focused sub-brand, "BMH Invest" (with "Book My Home Commercial" as the descriptor), so its warm, family-oriented "Happiness Begins Now" residential identity stays intact while the commercial arm speaks the language of yield, footfall and capital appreciation. The core buyer persona is the Tricity HNI or NRI investor and the local business-owner aged 35-55 who already trusts BMH for homes and now wants a tangible, rent-generating asset (an SCO, booth or retail floor) rather than volatile equity. Positioning should reframe the brand promise from emotional comfort to financial confidence: "Where your money begins to work" or "Invest where the footfall is." The three trust signals to show prominently on every commercial listing are: (1) the verified RERA registration number with a one-click link to the Punjab RERA portal, (2) transparent, source-cited pricing and any developer-advertised assured-return terms clearly labelled as such (never implied as guaranteed by BMH), and (3) location intelligence proof such as frontage width, road/corridor name, catchment density and nearby anchors, ideally with a footfall or rental-comparable data point. Together these signals let a residential brand credibly cross-sell commercial inventory without diluting its consumer-friendly reputation.
